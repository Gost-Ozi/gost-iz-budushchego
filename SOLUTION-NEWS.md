## 1. Executive Summary
Traditional news aggregators rely on "Attention Economy" (clicks/time-on-page). This creates algorithmic bias, clickbait, and promotes AI-generated information pollution.

**Solution-News** is a module that applies the **Solution-OS** core to media:
- **Filtering**: Not by engagement, but by *Coherence* and *Verification*.
- **Integrity**: Every piece of critical data is cross-referenced via the Trust Layer.
- **Goal**: Deliver precise, actionable information at the reader’s tempo, preventing burnout.

## 3. The Value Proposition

### Problem: The "Noise" Crisis
1. **AI Sprawl**: Exponential growth of low-quality, AI-generated content.
2. **Context Loss**: Information is shredded, missing the "why" and "what next".
3. **Manipulation**: Algorithmic ranking prioritizes emotional triggers over facts.

### Solution: The "Coherent News" Standard
1. **Fact-First Ranking**: Content ranking is determined by the `Trust Score` of the source + `Verification DAO` approval.
2. **Tempo-Controlled**: Uses `AI Coherence Engine` to prevent information overload. News doesn't push you; it waits for your cognitive capacity.
3. **Actionable Insights**: AI transforms raw data into summaries that lead to solutions, not just outrage.
